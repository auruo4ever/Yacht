Introducing Yacht Marina

Maldives Yacht Marina System provides a user-friendly website. On this website, users can find the information about marina rental, renting of warehouse premises for the large yacht equipment and specific yacht services.

The system contains the scheme of the marina. It shows which place is available for parking and its price. Users can book these places via special form. Yacht owners can also find the necessary contacts for the nearest yacht service there and general information about the yacht marina.
